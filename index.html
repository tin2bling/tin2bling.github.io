About Us

We are a family business coming to help your family get rid of their old metal.

We are around every day collecting from the kerbside or ground floor and are awaiting for your request. Just make contact for a quick and speedy response.

  • Any scrap metal collected not just white goods (except fridges/freezers) - old car parts, radiators, boilers, corrugated sheets. So long as it fits in the van we will take it away for you from the kerbside.
  • Please contact us via messenger or text to 07396521989 and we will do our best to collect the next day.
  • If you are not sure just contact us and we will advise if we can collect. A photo would help if you have a pile.
